I play a mix of traditional and contemporary songs.

Consider adding an island flavor to your next party, event, or venue!

Memorable Occasions

Adding island sounds will give a special touch to your event or party and make it memorable.

Consider steel drum music for Private parties, Festivals, Restaurants, Clubs, and Wedding receptions.

I live and perform in the Columbus, Ohio area.

I perform on the side, so weeknight availability is usually limited to after 6pm.

Music For Venues

I can play venues accommodating crowds up to 150 people.

I require only a single 110v outlet, at least 4'x6' of flat ground or floor space, and shade if the event is outside.

I have a portable battery system and canopy available for a fee - see Booking info for details.

I play with professionally produced backing tracks over a high-quailty Bose® PA system.

I am now equipped with LED wash lights, so go ahead and book me for that evening event!
