I'm Tony Schmitt.

I play the steel drum, an instrument from Trinidad and Tobago.

Find out more

I play a mix of traditional and contemporary songs.

Consider adding an island flavor to your next party, event, or venue!

Central Ohio

I live and perform in the Columbus, Ohio area.

I play on the side, so weekday availability is usually limited to after 6pm.

Memorable

Adding island sounds will give a special touch to your event or party and make it memorable.

Consider steel drum music for:

  • Private parties
  • Festivals
  • Restaurants
  • Clubs
  • Wedding receptions

Venues

I can play venues accommodating crowds up to 150 people easily.

I require only a single 110v outlet, 4'x6' of floor space, and shade if the event is outside.

I play with professionally produced backing tracks over a high-quailty Bose® PA system.

Steel drum music can liven up any party.
